1. The 5 AI Solo Business Archetypes That Actually Pay

For a solo beginner, these 5 are realistic, low-overhead, and sellable right now.

Model 1

AI Workflow Automator

Build boring, money-saving automations. n8n, Make.com, Zapier + GPT/Claude.

Offer: “Auto-qualify leads → CRM”, “Invoice extraction to QuickBooks”

Price: $1,500–$5,000 setup + $300–$900/mo

Model 2

Vibecoded Micro-SaaS

Tiny, single-purpose SaaS for a hyper-niche. Built with Cursor, Bolt, Lovable, Replit.

Offer: Notion-to-LinkedIn carousel generator for realtors. AI SOP writer for dental clinics.

Price: $19–$79/mo. 50 users = real income.

Model 3

AI Creative SMMA

One-person creative agency. AI UGC ads, short-form video scripts, thumbnails.

Tools: Runway, HeyGen, ElevenLabs, OpusClip

Price: $1,000–$3,500/mo retainers

Model 4

AI Auditor & Implementer

Audit a business, find 5 AI wins, deliver a roadmap, implement the first 2.

Offer: “AI Ops Audit - $1,500 flat. I find you $5k/mo in savings”

Price: High trust, high ticket

Model 5

AI Copy / Script Engine

Become a business’s AI-powered writing brain. Newsletters, cold email, YouTube scripts, sales pages.

Tools: Claude, ChatGPT + your own custom GPTs

Price: $800–$2,500/mo per client

Your stack for any path:
ChatGPT/Claude + Make/n8n + Cursor/Bolt + CapCut/OpusClip + Notion. ~$100/mo in tools.

7. The 7-Day Validation Sprint

Don’t build for 3 months. Sell in 7 days. This works for ALL 5 models.

1

Day 1–2 — Niche & Offer

"I build AI [Workflow / Ads / Scripts] for [Specific Niche] that get them [Specific Outcome] in 14 days, or free." Example: "AI lead qualification for roofing companies that books 5 extra jobs/mo."

2

Day 3 — Build 1 Demo

Not a product. One killer Loom demo. Fake data is fine. Show before/after.

3

Day 4–5 — 50 Outreach Messages

Cold email, IG DMs, LinkedIn, local Facebook groups. Problem → Demo link → 15-min call CTA. You need 5 replies, not 50 clients.

4

Day 6 — 3 Free Pilot Audits

Audit their process live for 15 mins. Give 1 free win. Then pitch: "$1,200 to build the full system, 50% upfront."

5

Day 7 — Close 1

One paying client at any price validates everything. Now build it.

9. Final Take: Stop Trying to Be Someone Else

The best AI solo business isn’t the trendiest one. It’s the one that fits your natural work style so you don’t burn out in week 3.

Introverted builder?

Don’t start an SMMA that needs daily client calls. Go build a Micro-SaaS.

People-person, no code?

Don’t force n8n for 2 months. Sell AI scripts and creative.

Pick ONE archetype. Pick ONE niche. Send 50 DMs this week.
Your personality didn’t choose your business. Your first paying client did.
